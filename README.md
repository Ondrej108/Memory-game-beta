## ğŸš€ How to Play

1. Click the **Start Game** button to begin
2. Click on any card to reveal its image
3. Try to find matching pairs by clicking two cards
4. If the cards match, they stay revealed
5. If they don't match, they flip back over
6. Find all 10 pairs before the 2-minute timer runs out
7. Use the **Reset Game** button to start over anytime
